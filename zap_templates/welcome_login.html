ABOUT ZAPYLE

Zapyle is a fun, easy and social way to shop like a pro. The best closets in India to buy from and your own to sell.

Zapyle offers you the most stylish closets in the country to shop from and helps you sell your own items that haven’t been getting a lot of love from you lately. Just list it for sale and off it goes. Once you receive the money – you can put that into buying the stuff that you really want. Giving your wardrobe a makeover has never been simpler!

Apart from being a fast and easy fashion marketplace, Zapyle is also a great place to discover the latest trends and styles. Once you’ve fed in your details like size, preferred style, body type, favourite brands, et cetera, the app suggests the best products you can shop for.
