FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What do I get from this membership?

With this membership, you become a valued member of The Zap Community - an exclusive community of premium and luxury fashion enthusiasts in India. Simply by paying a fixed price every month, you get access to the latest premium, luxury and über luxury fashion at incredible deals. You can use 1 bag for a month from the collection you subscribe to and it can be exchanged for another at the end of the month. Now flaunting your bags is just a click away. You can check out the plans above.
Who can join Zap Subscribe?

Currently, we are offering this subscription service only in Bangalore. But sit tight; we are expanding to other places soon! If you’re not from Bangalore, write to us at hello@zapyle.com, and we’ll try to make it happen for you.
Are there any hidden costs?

Not at all. With Zap Subscribe, what you like is what you get, and what you see is what you pay.
How is it better than renting?

In a lot of ways. With Zap Subscription, we didn’t want to provide you just another rental service, we wanted to give you special privileges and a unique experience.

By being a member of the Zap Community, you get access to multiple items in our inventory. Each item you get can be kept with you for as long as you want (until your subscription ends), and if you don’t really like it or are bored of it, you can choose to exchange it with something else from our vast collection. Like something so much that you want to buy it? We’ll throw in a special, irresistible discount for you and make this a whole package!
Can I end the subscription at any point?

Of course. We’ll be sad to let you go, but if for any reason, you want to end the subscription, you’re free to do so. The money for the cancelled period will be refunded to you. But considering our guarantee in providing you the best, exclusive service of the Zap Community, we bet you won’t be going anywhere!
