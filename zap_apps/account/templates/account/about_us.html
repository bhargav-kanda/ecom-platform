Story

The inspiration behind Zapyle was to find a solution to the universal problem of having nothing to wear - or managing our closets.

Millions of Indian women stock their closets with stuff worth over a billion dollars every year, and still have nothing to wear. We see you nod in agreement!

We only use 20% of our closet 80% of the time. True story.

That's a ton of money and inventory just lying around!

Zapyle is here to keep your closet stay updated and make you rich in the process. You can discover, sell and buy fashion here, and start a revolving closet! But most importantly, Zapyle is a fun community of incredible women who love fashion.

Luxury available at attractively marked down prices... Femina

Vision

Every product will have 5 owners in its lifetime.

Selling fashion will be as easy as buying it and we'll soon see the huge potential and great value lying hidden in our closets. It won't be clothes or bags or shoes that we own, but memories that are meant to be passed on to another when our time with them is done. Our fashion will live not just through us, but will have 5 lives instead of one.

Zapyle celebrates that vision.

Zapyle encourages collaborative shopping, building a product for fashion-conscious women Economic Times

Mission

And our mission is to deliver love to those million women, one closet at a time.
