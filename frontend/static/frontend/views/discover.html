Trendy

There's no such thing as a 'style guide' when we speak of the trendy aesthetic; if you're a trend hog, then you're probably up to date with what's making it to the runways, what's trending online (hello normcore!), on Instagram - even what Kim K wore to lunch at Nobu!

Boho

Flowing maxis, fringe, music festivals, and flowers in your hair - all the touchstones of a freespirited boho dresser. Aside from Coachella, channeling the hippie-chic vibe is perfect for occasions ranging from barbeque parties to a holiday wardrobe. Whether you're a whims-and-fancy dresser or all out boho, this one's quite a fun aesthetic to play with.

Classic

The classic school of fashion is one of timeless and tasteful style, slightly more conservative and mature than others, but far from boring. At first glance, if you see your wardrobe dominated by neutrals - blacks, beiges, whites, grays, navy blue; and your favourite pieces are all clean, straight silhouettes, you most probably belong in the classic style brigade.

Chic

A chic sense of style is somewhere between classy and trendy. It generally consists of timeless style staples like shirts and dresses in basic colours, tan totes and black pumps, but will also have a good blend of the hottest trends, whether a graphic printed cape or a number jersey tee.
